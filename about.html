About Us

At DFAC PACK, we're committed to providing high-quality, disposable items for all of your food service needs. Our selection of products includes everything from paper plates and cups to aluminum trays and foil. We know that convenience and affordability are important factors for our customers, which is why we offer competitive pricing and bulk options. Our products are perfect for catering businesses, event planners, or anyone looking to host a gathering without the hassle of cleanup. Sustainability is important to us, which is why we offer eco-friendly options such as compostable plates and biodegradable utensils. We pride ourselves on our excellent customer service and timely delivery. Whether you're hosting a large event or simply need disposable items for a family dinner, DFAC PACK has you covered. We're constantly updating our selection to include new and innovative products that meet our high standards for quality and durability. Thank you for choosing DFAC PACK for all of your disposable item needs. We look forward to serving you!
